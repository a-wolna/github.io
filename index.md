## About
I'm a postdoctoral researcher working with [Evelina Fedorenko](https://evlab.mit.edu/people/evelina-fedorenko/) at the McGovern Institute for Brain Research at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

Most broadly, my work focuses on the language system in the brain. Within this field, I am particularly interested in:
- the relationship between high-level language and speech articulation systems
- the characterization of the language system, including non-canonical language regions and the role of lateralization and co-lateralization in language and speech processing
- the organization of the language system in bilinguals, and the engagement of additional domain-general resources in bilingual speech production
